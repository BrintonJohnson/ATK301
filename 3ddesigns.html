3D Designs

These are some 3D Designs I made in Expanded Media I. I took that class at Illinois State University in the Spring of 2020.

Whale

This is a wooden whale sculpture that I made in Expanded Media. I designed the whale in Illustrator and separated it into layers. Then, I cut it with a laser cutter and super glued it together.

Ocean Waves

This is a wooden ocean sculpture that I made in Expanded Media. I designed the ocean's parts in Illustrator. I used lattice hinge patterns to give the wood flexibility. Then, I cut the parts out with a laser cutter and fit the pieces together.
