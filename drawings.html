A Collection of Works in Creative Technology

Drawings

These are drawings I made in the 2D Design class and Drawing I at Lincoln Land Community College. I took 2D Design in the Fall of 2018 and Drawing I in the Fall of 2019.

Building

This is a drawing of a building with wild, organic shapes transitioning into a plain, static building. I made this in Drawing I. My professor gave me a painting and told me to make something new from it, and this was the result.

Bird On Picasso

This is a drawing of a bird on a Picasso painting. I made this in Drawing I. The assignment tested our ability to render and draw what we see.

Falling Cards

This is a drawing of gradient cards falling. I made this in Drawing I. This assignment was to teach us gradient shading and drawing lightly for objects that are meant to be farther away.

Drapery

This is a drawing of drapes. I made it in Drawing I. The assignment tested our ability to render and draw what we see.

Self Portrait

This is a drawing of myself. I made it in Drawing I. It is based on a picture of my face and hand on a photocopier. The black background was made from charcoal.

Overlapping Outlines

This is a drawing of shape outlines made by rubbing charcoal from paper cutouts of each shape. It was made in Drawing I. There are a total of five unique shapes, but they are rotated randomly throughout the piece.

Pattern Transition

This is a drawing of two patterns connecting in the middle; the diamond pattern fades into the swirl pattern, and the swirl pattern overlaps the diamond pattern. I made this piece in Drawing I. I was given two patterns to base my drawing's patterns on, which I put in the lower-left and upper-right corners. Then, I drew outward from there.

Buisness and Butterflies

This is a drawing of butterflies inside the outline of a woman with business graphs and charts in the background. I made it in Drawing I. The assignment tested our ability to render and draw what we see.

Glow

This is a drawing of the word, GLOW. I made it in Drawing I. It shows representations of what the word, glow, means.

Zodiac Bull

This is a drawing of a bull made from kitchen utensils and lids. I drew this in Drawing I. First, I organized items from my kitchen to look like my zodiac sign, which is a bull. I took a picture and then drew the bull based on that picture. Finally, I added a bull pattern in the background, which is based on a famous bull cave drawing.

Household Collage

This drawing shows an assortment of household items. This was my Drawing I final project. I was given a list of things I could draw for this assignment. I gathered most of these items and drew them individually. The photograph and watch were drawn based on pictures, though.

Radial Symmetry

This is a drawing of a design with horizontal and vertical symmetry. This was made in my 2D Design class. All of the shapes were made symmetrical, but the shading within some of the shapes make those shapes look like they could rotate around the center of the image.

Elements of Design

This is a set of small drawings that show combinations of the elements of design. This was made for my 2D Design class. The assignment was to draw eight designs, each based on the combination of two of the elements of design. The first picture is frequency and variation; the second is repetition and static; the third is scale and overlapping; the fourth is direction and movement; the fifth is geometric and negative/positive space; the sixth is horizontal and symmetry; the seventh is vertical and balance; and the eighth is organic and weight.
