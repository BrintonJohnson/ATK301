Videos

Here are some of the video projects I have worked on for Digital Animation and Film Production I at Lincoln Land and Video II at Illinois State. I took Animation and Film Production in the Fall of 2018 and Video II in the Spring of 2020.

Motion Text

This was a video I made for my Video II class. It is a clip from a video game commentary video on YouTube. I animated the text to make it seem like the commentator's text was interacting with the game's text.

Moving Shape

This was a video I made for my Video II class. I made it in After Effects as a cool shape animation video.

The Shot

This was one of the first videos I made for my Video II class. It was made to show examples of different types of camera shots.

Illinois State Narration

This is another video I made in my Video II class. It is meant to be a short documentary of Illinois State University based on some interesting facts I had researched. It's not that exciting, but I made it to show how I might direct a documentary film like this. I took all of the shots on campus and in downtown Bloomington.

Casino Fight

This was my final project for Video II. I was allowed to make anything, as long as I used some filming and editing techniques we learned earlier in the semester. I decided to make a funny video with special effects and a green screen.

The Midnight Visitor

This was my final project in my Film Production class. I made a horror film because I had a limited number of actors for this film. I filmed, directed, and edited the video myself.

Alien Lands On Earth

This was the first animation I made for my Animation class. I created the alien in Illustrator and animated him in ToonBoom. This was when I learned about looping backgrounds and squash-and-stretch animation.

Steve the Alien

This was an animation I made in my Animation class. It is the first character animation video I have made. I also made this with ToonBoom.

Exploding Sun Animation

This was another short animation I made in my Animation class. It is the first 3D animation I have created. I used Cinema 4D to animate the video.
